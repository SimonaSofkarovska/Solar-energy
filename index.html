Steca Solarix 2525, 4040

Solar charge controllers Steca Solarix 2525 and 4040 are newly developed products based on the internationally renowned controller family StecaPR. With increased module and load current, the controllers are now also suitable for even larger systems. Use of the latest charging technologies combined with state of charge determination enables optimal battery maintenance and module power monitoring. The state of charge is represented visually as a level meter. Data such as voltage, current, and state of charge can also be displayed digitally as figures on the display. Moreover, the controller has an energy meter that can be reset by the user.
